I am a Senior UX Designer at BP3 Global.

Through a focus on custom enterprise software, I help organizations improve the systems their teams rely on every day. I use agile design to simplify complex workflows, which creates operational efficiencies.

I also lean on business process modeling notation (BPMN), collaboration, and problemsolving, to reach stakeholder alignment, while always being sure to put the users first.

Having experience leading designers and developers alike, as well as facilitating many workshops and presentations with clients, I am comfortable with finding appropriate solutions, no matter the challenge at hand.

Outside of UX, I enjoy cycling, photography, and exploring urban spaces near and far. I live with my wife and two kids in Fishtown, Philadelphia.
